Napoleon was very short

FICTION

Napoleon was of average height for his time

The idea that Napoleon Bonaparte was unusually short is a myth. He was recorded as being around 5 feet 2 inches tall in French measurements, which translates to about 5 feet 6 inches in modern terms.

This was average for a man in the early 19th century. The misconception likely arose from a mix-up between French and British measurement systems, as well as British propaganda that sought to portray him as a diminutive figure to undermine his reputation.
