Humans only use 10% of their brains

FICTION

Humans use virtually all parts of their brains

The claim that humans only use 10% of their brains is a myth. Brain imaging studies show that nearly all parts of the brain have some level of activity, even during simple tasks or at rest. Different areas of the brain are specialized for functions like movement, memory, emotions, and sensory processing, and they work together in complex ways.

The misconception may have originated from misunderstandings of early neurological research or was exaggerated in popular culture.
