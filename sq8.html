Goldfish have a three-second memory

FICTION

Goldfish can remember things for months

The belief that goldfish have a three-second memory is a myth. Studies have shown that goldfish are capable of learning and remembering tasks, recognizing their owners, and recalling information for months.

They can be trained to navigate mazes, press levers for food, and even associate certain sounds with feeding time. This demonstrates a level of intelligence and memory far beyond the three-second misconception.
